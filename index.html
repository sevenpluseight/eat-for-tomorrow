About

Welcome to Eat For Tomorrow 🎮🍎 — a 14-day food adventure where your choices change your Health ❤️, Money 💰, and the Environment 🌳.

  • Healthy food → ❤️ + points, 🌳 + points
  • Junk food → ❤️ - points, 💰 - points, 🌳 - points
  • Sweets → ❤️ - points
  • Fancy meals → 💰 - points
  • Surprise food → random + or - points

Play through all 14 days and see who you become — 🌱 a healthy eco-hero, 🎂 a cake lover, or something in between!

❤️

Features

🎮 14-Day Food Journey

Face two weeks of quirky food choices, from greasy burgers to suspicious leftovers. Every meal shapes your story.

🎯 Mini-Games Madness

From Quick Click to Memory Match, Eco-Plate, and Supermarket Dash — test your reflexes, memory, and choices in fast-paced fun.

🥗 Multiple Endings

End as an eco hero, broke but healthy, or a fast-food cautionary tale. Your diet decides your destiny.
